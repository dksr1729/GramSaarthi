## FINAL ANSWER
### Ayushman Bharat Scheme Summary
- **What is Ayushman Bharat?**
Ayushman Bharat, also known as Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), is a health insurance scheme launched by the Government of India.
- **Who is Eligible?**
- Families listed in the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011.
- Workers in the unorganized sector not covered by other schemes.
- **Coverage:**
- Up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.
- Includes pre- and post-hospitalization expenses.
- Covers 1,393 medical procedures.
- **How to Apply:**
- Visit your nearest Public Health Centre.
- Carry your SECC 2011 data and identity proof.
- Get an Ayushman Bharat card if eligible.
- **Benefits:**
- Free and cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals.
- No need to pay out-of-pocket for covered treatments.
- **Exclusions:**
- Certain high-cost procedures and treatments.
- Certain pre-existing conditions may not be covered initially.
- **Contact:**
- For more details, visit the official website or contact local health authorities.
