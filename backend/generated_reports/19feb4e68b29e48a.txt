## FINAL ANSWER
### Cattle Shed Scheme Summary
- **Objective**: To support farmers, particularly Below Poverty Line (BPL) farmers, in constructing and improving cattle sheds.
- **Key Features**:
- **Subsidies**:
- 75% subsidy for BPL farmers to construct cattle sheds.
- 25% merit subsidy for BPL farmers for cattle shed construction.
- **Financial Support**:
- Rs. 3000 subsidy per cattle shed.
- Subsidy for purchasing milking machines and chop cutters at 75%.
- **Additional Benefits**:
- Assistance in setting up mini dairy units for unemployed youth with 75% subsidy.
- Support for setting up turkey farms and broiler farms at 75% subsidy.
- Subsidy for purchasing day-old chicks for commercial poultry farming.
- **Implementation**:
- Managed by the Animal Husbandry Department and Animal Welfare.
- Part of broader schemes like Rashtriya Gokul Mission and National Livestock Mission.

ELIGIBILITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
### ELIGIBILITY
**Who is eligible for the Cattle Shed Scheme?**
- **Eligible Participants:**
- Below Poverty Line (BPL) farmers
- Farmers with owned, rented, or leased sheds
**Exclusions:**
- Non-BPL farmers
- Individuals or entities without any form of shed (owned, rented, or leased)
