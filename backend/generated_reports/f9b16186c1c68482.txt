### Aayushman Bharat Scheme Eligibility Criteria
- **Objective**: To provide health insurance coverage to economically disadvantaged families.
- **Eligible Population**:
- Families listed in the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011.
- Rural families.
- Urban poor not covered under health insurance schemes by other government or private insurers.
- **Coverage**:
- Up to 500,000 INR per family per year for secondary and tertiary healthcare.
- **Identification**:
- Beneficiaries are identified through a unique health card.
- **Exclusions**:
- Individuals paying income tax.
- Individuals from higher income groups.
- Members of certain professional categories (doctors, engineers, etc.).
- **States and Union Territories**:
- All states and union territories are covered under the scheme.
- **Enrollment**:
- Eligible families can enroll at public health facilities or through designated centers.
