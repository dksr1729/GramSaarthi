## FINAL ANSWER
### What is PMAY?
- **Full Form**: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)
- **Objective**: To provide affordable housing to urban and rural poor.
- **Key Features**:
- Aimed at the economically weaker sections and low-income groups.
- Includes both new construction and repair/renovation of existing homes.
- Beneficiaries can choose to receive a subsidy directly or opt for interest-free loans.
- Targets completion of 11 million housing units by 2022.
- **Eligibility**:
- Primarily for those who do not own a house as of a specified date.
- Income criteria vary based on urban and rural areas.
- **Implementation**:
- Managed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.
- Involves collaboration with state governments and local bodies.
- **Benefits**:
- Financial assistance for building, enhancing, or buying a house.
- Improved living conditions and reduced housing shortage.

ELIGIBILITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
### ELIGIBILITY
#### What is PMAY?
- **Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)**: A government initiative aimed at providing affordable housing to urban and rural poor.
#### Who is Eligible?
- **Economically Weaker Sections (EWS)**:
- Individuals whose annual family income is below ₹300,000.
- Families owning less than 10 square meters of residential space in notified municipalities or less than 20 square meters in non-notified areas.
- **Low-Income Groups (LIG)**:
- Annual family income below ₹300,000.
- Families owning less than 20 square meters of residential space in notified municipalities or less than 40 square meters in non-notified areas.
- **Middle-Income Groups (MIG-I and MIG-II)**:
- MIG-I: Annual family income between ₹300,000 and ₹600,000.
- Families owning less than 20 square meters in notified municipalities or less than 40 square meters in non-notified areas.
- MIG-II: Annual family income between ₹600,000 and ₹1,800,000.
- Families owning less than 40 square meters in notified municipalities or less than 80 square meters in non-notified areas.
#### Exclusions:
- **Current Home Owners**: Individuals who already own a pucca (permanent) house.
- **Beneficiaries of Other Housing Schemes**: Those who have already benefited from other government housing schemes.
- **High-Income Individuals**: Those with an annual family income above ₹1,800,000.

### FINANCIAL DETAILS
#### PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana)
- **Subsidy/Assistance/Loan Support:**
- **Subsidy:**
- Provided for the construction/repair of houses.
- Amount varies based on location (urban/rural) and size of the house.
- Typically up to INR 2.67 lakh for urban areas and INR 1.50 lakh for rural areas.
- **Eligibility:**
- Primarily for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) and Low-Income Groups (LIG).
- Middle-Income Group I (MIG-I) and Middle-Income Group II (MIG-II) also eligible with certain conditions.
- **Loan Support:**
- Interest subsidy on loans up to INR 6 lakh for MIG-I and INR 9 lakh for MIG-II.
- Facilitated through various banks and financial institutions.
- **Limits:**
- Subsidy limits vary by location and income group.
- Urban areas: EWS (up to INR 1.50 lakh), LIG (up to INR 2.67 lakh).
- Rural areas: EWS (up to INR 1.30 lakh), LIG (up to INR 1.50 lakh).
- **Additional Support:**
- Interest subsidy for MIG-I and MIG-II categories.
- Support for slum dwellers and special categories like disabled and widows.
