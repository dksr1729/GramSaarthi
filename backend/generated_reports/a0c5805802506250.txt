FINAL ANSWER
Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a government scheme aimed at providing affordable housing to urban and rural poor. Key points include:
- **Objective**: To ensure "Housing for All" by 2022.
- **Target Group**: Low-income and economically weaker sections (EWS) in both urban and rural areas.
- **Components**:
- **Credit Linked Subsidy**: Financial assistance for beneficiaries to build or buy homes.
- **Affordable Rental Housing Complexes (ARHC)**: Development of rental housing for economically weaker sections.
- **In-Situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)**: Upgrading existing slum areas.
- **Benefits**:
- Direct benefit transfers to beneficiaries.
- Focus on sustainable and inclusive housing solutions.
- **Implementation**: Managed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs in collaboration with state governments.

ELIGIBILITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
### ELIGIBILITY
#### What is PMAY
- PMAY stands for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, a government scheme aimed at providing affordable housing to the urban poor.
#### Who is eligible?
- **Eligibility Criteria:**
- Urban households whose annual income is less than INR 3 lakhs.
- Urban households whose annual income is between INR 3 lakhs and INR 6 lakhs and do not own a pucca house in their name.
- Households living in kutcha houses, partial pucca houses, or are living in the same premises as the landowner.
- Households living in dilapidated kutcha or partial pucca houses.
#### Exclusions:
- Households who own more than one residential house.
- Households who own a pucca house in their name.
- Households whose annual income exceeds INR 6 lakhs.
