**What is PMAY**

- **PMAY** stands for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana.
- It is a housing scheme launched by the Government of India.
- The primary goal is to provide affordable housing to urban and rural poor.
- The scheme aims to achieve "Housing for All" by 2022.

**Key Points:**

- **Target Group:** Low-income and economically weaker sections (EWS) of the society.
- **Financial Assistance:** Provides financial support for the construction or purchase of houses.
- **Implementation:** Managed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.
- **Benefits:** Includes access to basic amenities like water, sanitation, and electricity.

Topic: What is PMAY

- **PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana)**: A scheme launched by the Government of India to provide affordable housing to urban and rural poor.
  
- **Subsidy/Assistance**:
  - **Financial Assistance**: Direct financial support provided to beneficiaries to help them build, improve, or buy a house.
  - **Interest Subsidy**: Beneficiaries can avail loans at a reduced interest rate to construct or buy a house.
  
- **Loan Support**:
  - **Eligible Loan Amount**: Varies based on the location and size of the house.
  - **Interest Subsidy Duration**: Typically for the entire loan tenure, up to a maximum of 15 years.
  
- **Limits**:
  - **Income Limit**: Varies by urban and rural areas; generally, beneficiaries with an annual family income below INR 6 lakh in urban areas and INR 3 lakh in rural areas are eligible.
  - **House Size**: Maximum built-up area of 90 sq. meters in urban areas and 120 sq. meters in rural areas.
