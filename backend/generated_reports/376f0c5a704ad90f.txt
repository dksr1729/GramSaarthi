### FINAL ANSWER
**Cattle Shed Scheme Summary:**
- **Objective:**
- To improve productivity of crossbred cattle.
- To reduce infertility and increase production.
- To select high yielding animals using improved technology.
- To upgrade local goat population using assisted reproductive technology.
- **Key Components:**
- **Subsidies:**
- 75% subsidy for the distribution of milch animals to Below Poverty Line (BPL) farmers.
- Subsidy for cattle feed at Rs.2/- per Kg. for Milch animals.
- Subsidy for pregnant animals at Rs.3/- per Kg.
- Merit subsidy of 25% for BPL farmers to construct cattle sheds.
- **Incentives:**
- 75% subsidy for the purchase of milking machines.
- 75% subsidy for the purchase of chop cutters.
- 75% subsidy for unemployed youth to purchase mini dairy units.
- **Implementation:**
- Managed by the Animal Husbandry Department and Animal Welfare.
- Part of the broader National Livestock Mission and other related schemes.
- **Achievements:**
- Distribution of milch animals to BPL farmers.
- Provision of subsidized feed and other inputs.
- Conduction of infertility control programs and camps.
- **Future Plans:**
- Expansion of mobile artificial insemination units.
- Introduction of new Key Village Units.
- Continued support for the construction of cattle sheds and other infrastructure.
